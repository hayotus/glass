COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know

How long does window repair usually take?
Most repairs are completed within 1–3 hours. For complex cases like full sash replacements, we provide a clear timeline upfront.
Can you repair foggy double-pane windows?
Absolutely. We replace the insulated glass unit (IGU) and restore thermal performance without replacing the entire frame.
Do you offer same-day emergency repair?
Yes – 24/7 emergency service for shattered windows, break-ins, or storm damage. Call our hotline for immediate dispatch.
Is window repair better than full replacement?
In most cases, repairing a broken seal or a cracked pane is cost-effective. We’ll assess and give honest advice.
What types of windows do you repair?
Single hung, double hung, casement, sliding, bay, bow, and skylights — both residential and commercial.
How much does window repair cost?
The cost depends on the damage type, window size, and materials. We always provide a free, honest estimate with transparent pricing before starting any work.
